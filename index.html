About Me

I'm an engineer working on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP and Computer Vision.
I’ve worked with various deep learning, machine learning, and computer vision libraries such as TensorFlow, Pytorch, OpenCV, Sckit-learn, and PIL. The products I developed are working on edge servers, GPU servers, security cameras, raspberry pi, nvidia jetson, iPhone, and MacBook. I use C, C++, Python, C# and Objective-C programming languages.

Practical

I’m a dedicated person who gets things done on time. I’ve been working on data science, machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision for over 5 years for solving challenges with high impact.

My latest works are on 3D Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Mobile Development, Unity Development, ANNs, CNNs, R-CNNs, GANs, Auto-encoders, NLP, Recommendation Systems, and Transfer Learning to realize projects including Segmentation, Classification, Hand Reconstruction, 3D Object Detection, 3D Synthetic Image Generation, Medical Image Analysis, Semantic Analysis, and Fraud Detection.

Theorical

I'm a PhD candidate who works on Explainable Artificial Intelligence. I’m extremely eager to continue my work with a Computer Vision and Machine Learning concentration. Along with my academic studies; I’ve given lectures as a project assistant for Digital Image Processing and Digital Video Processing lectures, and worked in two laboratories focusing on Human-Robot Interaction, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning and working interdisciplinary with the Department of Mechanical Engineering and Faculty of Medicine. All in all, I’m a self-motivated person, a lover of science, art, and making music.
