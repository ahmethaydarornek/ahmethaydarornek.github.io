About Me

I'm an engineer working on Deep Learning and Computer Vision.

Theoretical

After performing projects on human-robot interaction and computer vision as BSc student, I’ve completed my master thesis on Thermal Imaging and Machine Learning. I have a strong theoretical and practical background on Data Science, Linear Algebra, Statistics, Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Computer Vision.

Practical

I'm a dedicated person who uses time effectively to achieve goals given. I've been working on robotics, computer vision and deep learning since my undergraduate education. My latest works are on ANNs, CNNs, R-CNNs, GANs, Auto-encoders, and Transfer Learning to realize projects including Segmentation, Classification, Image Generating, and Object Detection.
